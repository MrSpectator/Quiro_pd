Why Now

  • Acceleration of Digital Education

    Post-COVID adoption & hybrid learning models created lasting demand for robust institutional systems and LMS integrations.

  • Policy & Funding Momentum

    Government & donor support for ICT in higher education; institutions are prioritizing digital transformation now to unlock funding and compliance benefits.

  • Large Untapped Market

    638+ institutions and ~2.3M students with low digital penetration — high upside to deploy modular solutions quickly.

  • Cost Pressure on Institutions

    Budget inefficiencies and paper-heavy workflows create immediate demand for automation that saves time and reduces costs.

Timing Signals

• Increased EdTech budgets from institutions and donors.

• Regulatory pushes for centralized admission & record systems (JAMB & NUC alignment).

• Mature cloud + local hosting options reducing deployment friction.

Conclusion: Now is the window to capture institutional pilots that scale into multi-year contracts.
