Solution Overview

Quiro is a modular, Nigeria-focused platform for universities:

Comprehensive suite of digital solutions designed specifically for Nigerian higher education.

JAMB CAPS Integration

Docket Generation

Automated creation of student reference booklets and institutional documentation.

NYSC Automation

Streamlined National Youth Service Corps documentation and processing.

Analytics & LMS

Robust learning management system with data-driven insights for student performance.

Hybrid Deployment

Flexible cloud or on-premise installation options catering to diverse infrastructure needs.
